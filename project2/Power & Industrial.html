Areas Of Expertise

Civil Architectural & Structural Works Pipelines Water Systems

Leveraging on its strengths and competencies in Civil Engineering & Construction, CGR offers its services for major Industrial , Process and Power Plants covering Site Grading & Levelling, Internal Roads & Drains, Services, Civil Foundations, Water Retaining Structures, Water Systems, Pipeline Fabrication & Laying, Construction of Non Plant Buildings and other associated structures.
GKC has also harnessed capabilities for Turnkey Implementation of Utility Size Solar Photo Voltaic Power Plants. GKC has developed a 10 MW Grid Connected Solar PV Power Plant at Bidar, Karnataka which is in commercial operation.

Projects Portfolio: Power:
  • Civil works for BoP of 2x500 MW TPP, Bhusawal, Maharashtra.
  • Civil works for BoP of 2x800 MW TPP, Krishnapatnam, A.P.
  • Grid Connected Solar PV power plant of 10 MW capacity at Bidar, Karnataka.
