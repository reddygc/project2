Human Resources - Learning key to Growth

Real assets of CGR are its people. To complement their commitment and efforts, the Company continuously engages, develops and rewards them to meet the
aspirations and expectations of all employees. Learning is the key to employees at all levels, be it learning the latest technology, adopting new construction
methodology, planning tools, project management, quality assurance or inspection & expediting
Fresh talent and abundant enthusiasm under the guidance of senior project managers gives the Company the winning edge in delivering projects in time. With
streamlined HR policy, career path at all levels is ensured to be a rewarding experience. Nurturing young talent for the future, CGR offers Internship programmes
for graduating engineers from reputed institutions. The Company has committed to provide such on-the-job training to students batches from BITS - Pilani Dubai
Campus on a regular basis covering all project and office functions.

HR Policy

CGR has adopted sound HR policy in tune with the Industry standards to hire and retain the best in human resources. The HR policy is in strict compliance with
the national and state statutory policies and is implemented across all sites, corporate and regional offices. Employee growth in the organization is linked to
performance and with availability of abundant opportunities, vertical growth is never a concern for the performing employees.
