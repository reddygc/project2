Power Transmission & Distribution

Areas Of Expertise

Substations Transmission Lines Pipelines Distribution

National focus on ‘Green Energy’, ‘Electricity for All’ and ‘Reliable & Continuous Power Supply to Consumers’ is driving vast and sustained business opportunities
in Power Transmission & Distribution sector. National Grid Projects, Green Power Corridors, Augmentation & Strengthening of Sub-Transmission & Distribution
Infrastructure, Renovation & Modernization of existing Sub- Stations & Lines, SCADA and Smart Grid etc., are the key areas offering business opportunities.
Indian Government has launched various facilitating schemes viz., R-APDRP, RGGVY, DDUGJY, NEF and Integrated Power Development Scheme (IPDS) aiming
at removing the bottlenecks in ensuring Reliable and Quality Power Supply to the consumers.
GKC foresaw the budding opportunities particularly in the Augmentation & Strengthening of Sub- Transmission & Distribution Infrastructure and secured awards
of projects in this space. We have substantial qualifications in the field and have secured a firm foothold in Power T&D.

Featured Project

Electrification works in Siwan District of Bihar on Turnkey basis under Rajiv Gandhi Grameen Vidyutikaran Yojana.

The Project is taken up as an implementation partner of NBPDCL (North Bihar Power Distribution Company Limited).
The Scope of Work includes Site Survey, Planning, Design, Engineering, Supply, Installation, Testing and Commissioning of all items/ materials required to
complete the Electrification works in Siwan District including Sub Stations, providing Service Connections to agricultural consumers and scattered habitants
including replacement/ Installation of Energy Meters spread all over the District.
