CGR Projects Limited offers comprehensive solutions in buildings covering Residential, Commercial, Institutional and Industrial buildings. It has to its credit,
execution of several such buildings for Public and Private sector clients. CGR has the wherewithal and expertise to execute projects ranging from Gated
communities sprawling over several hectares to multi storeyed buildings. One of our ongoing projects involves towers containing 3 Basements + G+ 14 storeys.
GKC also offers solutions in Pre-Engineered Buildings, Pre-cast and other new construction technologies.

Featured Projects: Buildings

Redevelopment of East Kidwai Nagar, Delhi

Construction of Type-II and Type-III Quarters along with other allied services in East Kidwai Nagar, Delhi. This Project is a large scale Residential Project
implemented by Ministry of Urban Development through NBCC (National Building Construction Corporation Limited) .
GKC is associated in execution of the Project which comprises construction of 23 towers, each consisting of 3 Basements with a Ground floor + 14 storey,
in East Kidwai Nagar, Delhi.
The Scope includes Construction of Type II and Type III Quarters along with Electrical, Mechanical & Plumbing works including Lifts, Gen-sets, Fire Fighting
System etc., it also includes providing Sewerage System, construction of non-tower areas for Drive Ways etc.

Integrated Township Project, Nellore, Krishnapatnam

Construction of Integrated Township and infrastructural works at 2 x 800 MW SDSTPS, at Nelatur, Nellore Dist., A. P.
The Project involves Construction of Integrated Township and associated infrastructural works for the employees of SDSTPS. The Township comprises Residential
and Non- Residential Buildings including Water Supply & Sanitary, Electrical Fittings and Infrastructure works like levelling & grading, construction of compound
wall, construction of drains and laying of roads etc
The non-residential buildings include School, Hospital, Auditorium, Indoor Games shed, Club Houses etc.
